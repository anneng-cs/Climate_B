Climate Issue

Bangledesh is a country located in South Asia that is currently facing a significant climate justice issue, namely, flooding due to rising sea levels and increased precipitation.

Severe Flooding

Flooding is a servere issue in Bangladesh that afftects millions of people each year. CLimate change has led to rising sea levels.

Food Insecurities

The floods have submered over 53,000 hectares of agricultural land, damaging the crops resulting with more than 4 million people at rish of food insecurities.
